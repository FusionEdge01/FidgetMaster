Privacy Policy for Fidget Master

Effective Date: July 2, 2025

FusionEdge ("we", "our", or "us") built the Fidget Master game. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect information when you use the game.

1. Information We Collect

a. Analytics Data

We use Firebase Analytics to collect non-personal information about how users interact with different game modes and features. This helps us improve the game experience based on aggregated player behavior.

  • Session duration
  • Game mode usage
  • Level completions
  • In-app behavior patterns
  • Device type, OS version, and general location (city-level)

This data is anonymous and cannot be used to personally identify you.

b. Advertising Data

We use Google AdMob to serve ads within the game. AdMob may collect information such as:

  • Device identifiers (e.g., advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • App usage data
  • Crash logs

This information may be used to deliver personalized or non-personalized ads based on your preferences and location (where applicable).

2. How We Use the Information

  • Analyze gameplay behavior and improve game modes and features
  • Fix bugs and optimize performance
  • Serve relevant advertisements
  • Ensure the app runs properly across different devices

We do not sell any personal information.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services, which have their own privacy policies:

  • Firebase Analytics (Google)
  • Google AdMob (Google Ads)

Fidget Master is designed for general audiences. However, we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your country). If we discover that we have collected such information, we will take steps to delete it.

Parents or guardians who believe their child has provided us with personal information can contact us at the email below.
